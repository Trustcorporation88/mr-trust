## TEMPLATE SCRIPT (Copy to Google Sheets)

```
=QUERY('Raw Data'!A:Z, "SELECT A, B, C, D where E > 0", 0)

Calculate Daily CAC:
=IF(C2=0, "N/A", B2/C2)

Calculate Weekly Revenue:
=SUMIFS(Revenue, Date, ">="&StartDate, Date, "<="&EndDate)

Calculate Monthly MRR:
=COUNTIF(Status, "Paid") * AvgMonthlyPrice
```

## ALERTS (Set up notifications)

**If any of these happen, take action immediately**:

🔴 **RED** - Stop everything and fix:
- Website down (check)
- CAC > R$ 500 (pause ads, fix landing page)
- Conversion rate dropped >50% (check GA, check copy)
- Email bouncing (check Mailchimp)

🟡 **YELLOW** - Investigate + plan fix:
- CAC trending up (optimize channels)
- Email open rate dropping (A/B test)
- Demo conversion < 20% (improve script)
- No trials 3 days in a row (increase ad spend)

🟢 **GREEN** - Scale:
- CAC < R$ 200 (increase ad budget)
- Demo conversion > 50% (book more demos)
- Email open > 40% (send more emails)
- Trial conversion > 10% (improve copy everywhere)
