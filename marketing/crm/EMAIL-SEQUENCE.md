## 💡 Email Best Practices

- **Personalization**: Use [FIRST_NAME], company insights
- **Length**: Max 150 words (email) or 300 words (proposal email)
- **CTA**: 1 primary CTA per email (avoid decision paralysis)
- **Timing**: Send 9-10 AM (opens higher)
- **A/B Test**: Subject line, body copy, CTA text
- **Mobile**: 50% email opens are mobile (keep short)
- **Tracking**: Enable open tracking, click tracking
