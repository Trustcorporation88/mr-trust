## ⚠️ Common Pitfalls (Avoid)

❌ **Mixing with OSINT** - Keep completely separate product  
❌ **Waiting for perfection** - Launch imperfect, optimize fast  
❌ **No tracking** - If you're not measuring, you're guessing  
❌ **All channels at once** - Focus on LinkedIn (40%) + Ads (10%) first  
❌ **No follow-up** - 50% of conversions happen in follow-up  
❌ **Bad email copy** - Use templates provided  
❌ **Ignoring data** - If something's not working after 3 days, change it
