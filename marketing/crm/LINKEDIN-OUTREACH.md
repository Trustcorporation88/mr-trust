## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

❌ Generic messages ("Let's connect" only) → Low response
❌ Sales pitch immediately → Ignored
❌ Send >50/day → LinkedIn suspends account
❌ All same template → Looks like bot
❌ No follow-up → 50% of deals lost in follow-up

✅ Personalized (2-3 lines) → 30%+ response
✅ Problem-first, solution-second → 2x reply rate
✅ 10-20/day max → 30 days of runway
✅ Mix of templates → Looks human
✅ Follow-up in <24h → 80% conversion
